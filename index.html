We are Alerta

We are at the center of a revolution, where businesses catering to a multicultural audience are set to make it big, as demographics tip in their favor. Alerta! is a nimble ad agency built for success in the multicultural age. Research, creativity and technology are at the core of our principles but so is value. In a time when advertisers must create an unprecedented amount of content to stay in their audience’s top of mind, we want our clients to know that their budget will go as far as it possibly can. Our process and personnel distribution allow for incredible efficiency.


Culture is our Recipe.

It goes beyond ethnicity and language. Culture is what makes us proud of being who we are. It’s the sum of attitudes and behaviors that connect us to a group. When communication involves our culture, we’re more receptive, engaged and enthusiastic. Alerta! has a unique approach to communication that we call Culture First! because identifying a soft spot in your target’s heart allows for higher engagement, increases ROI and minimizes churn.

The Alerta Way

  • Assess – We do a deep dive into the current situation of the brand and its market
  • Leverage – We establish the brand’s opportunities for cultural connections (content, touch points, schedule)
  • Execute – We roll out the campaign and start measuring the impact
  • Refine – We optimize the creative based on social listening and sales data
  • Track – We gather and analyze data and feedback while the campaign is active
  • Amplify – We advance the objectives based on new opportunities discovered
